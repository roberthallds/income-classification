Summary and Overall Takeaways¶

  • Model achieved an accuracy score of approximately 82%.
  • Rates of higher education achievements are most highly positively correlated with income being in the upper income bracket while rates of elementary to junior-high school maximal education levels are most negatively correlated with income being in the upper income bracket.
  • ROC-AUC score is at a moderate 85% (ROC_AUC = 0.85), indicating that the model predicts positives and negatives somewhat accurately -- better than random guessing (ROC_AUC = 0.5) but does not predict income level perfectly (ROC_AUC = 1.0).
